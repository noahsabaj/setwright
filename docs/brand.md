# Brand usage

Setwright's release identity is monochrome: the Setwright wordmark, a black or
white field, and the line **Write papers, not TeX.** Splash, onboarding, store,
and release artwork should preserve that high-contrast treatment.

The working application uses system sans-serif type, a restrained neutral
palette, and one blue interaction accent, with light, dark, and high-contrast
themes. Product controls must remain legible without brand artwork and cannot
use color as the only signal.

The supplied identity is reproduced as checked-in monochrome vector lockups in
[`assets/brand/`](../assets/brand/). Typography must be converted to reviewed
outlines and platform store/raster exports approved before public packaging;
the checked-in lockup is not evidence of trademark clearance or registration.

Name and identity permissions are defined in [TRADEMARK.md](../TRADEMARK.md).
